Tykhe is an innovative organizational website for students that lets you organize your work effortlessly

We all know that students are currently going through the toughest times of their lives. The pandemic has greatly affected students and the way they do their work. Usually it was a simple, get your books from your locker and go type of situation, but now everything is online. All your classes are online, notifications from teachers are online, even your notes are online. The downside is that most of these are tough to remember. So we created a solution. It's called Tykhe. Tykhe is an organizational tool that has been highly optimized for students. You can log and have all the info for your online classes organized all in one place!
